Rustic Guacamole

Photo by Tessa Rampersad on Unsplash

About this Recipe:

Who doesn't like guacamole? Ours comes with a little kick to start your meal off right!

Equipment Needed:

  • Chef's knife
  • Cutting board
  • Mixing bowl

Ingredients Needed:

  • 3 large avocados
  • 1 tomato
  • 3 green onions
  • 2 limes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 bunch of cilantro
  • Hot sauce

Directions:

  1. Remove seeds and pulp from tomato and roughly chop
  2. Thinly slice the green onions
  3. Peel avocados
  4. Add avocado, tomato, green onion, cilantro, and hot sauce to mixing bowl
  5. Slice limes in half and squeeze the juice into the mixing bowl
  6. Mash/mix the ingredients into a good paste
  7. Salt & pepper to taste
  8. Serve and enjoy
