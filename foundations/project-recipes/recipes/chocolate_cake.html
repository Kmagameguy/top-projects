Flourless Chocolate Cake

Photo by Pushpak Dsilva on Unsplash

About this Recipe:

Cake for the gluten-sensitive! While this recipe doesn't use flour, it still delicious.

Equipment Needed:

  • 8 in. springform pan
  • Saucepan
  • Heat-proof bowl (or second, smaller sauce pan)
  • Measuring cups
  • Mixing bowl
  • Mixing spoon
  • Toothpicks

Ingredients Needed:

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, plus more for greasing the pan
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • (OPTIONAL) whipped cream or ice cream for serving

Directions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Butter the bottom and sides of the 8 in. pan
  3. Simmer a couple inches of water on medium heat in the large sauce pan
  4. Set heat-proof bowl into sauce pan and pour chocolate chips into the bowl
  5. Stir the chocolate chips frequently, until they are a smooth blend
  6. Turn off the heat and add butter to the bowl
  7. Stir butter and chocolate mix until smooth
  8. Add cocoa powder to the bowl and stir until smooth
  9. Remove bowl from saucepan
  10. Stir in the sugar until fully blended
  11. Add eggs to bowl, beating each one before adding the next
  12. Stir in the vanilla extract
  13. Pour mixture into the prepared pan, smooth the top
  14. Bake for about 40-50 minutes. A toothpick through the center of the cake should come out with crumbs attached
  15. Remove pan from oven and cool on a rack
  16. Remove cake from pan after a few minutes, slice, and serve. Add whipped cream or ice cream if you have it!
